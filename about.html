Research

03 / Research

I work on computer vision with a focus on fine-grained perception and generative modeling. Recently, I've been exploring diffusion-based methods and adapter-based finetuning (LoRA) for controllable synthesis—especially in medical imaging.

Fine-grained Computer Vision

I'm interested in recognition and detection settings where visual differences are subtle, and robustness/evaluation are as important as raw accuracy.

Generative Modeling

I build controllable generative systems (diffusion + LoRA, flow models) for data synthesis and model improvement, with an emphasis on realism and downstream utility.
