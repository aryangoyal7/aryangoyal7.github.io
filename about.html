Background

01 / Background

I’m currently an undergraduate student at IIT Bombay. I like building ML systems end-to-end: from data and training to evaluation and deployment. I’m especially drawn to problems where small details matter (fine-grained recognition and medical imaging).

Research

02 / Research

I work on computer vision with a focus on fine-grained perception and generative modeling. Recently, I’ve been exploring diffusion-based methods and adapter-based finetuning (LoRA) for controllable synthesis—especially in chest radiographs.

Fine-grained computer vision

I’m interested in recognition and detection settings where visual differences are subtle, and robustness/evaluation are as important as raw accuracy.

Generative modeling for perception

I build controllable generative systems (diffusion + LoRA) for data synthesis and model improvement, with an emphasis on realism and downstream utility.

Hobbies

03 / Hobbies

When I’m not working, I enjoy building things (tools, small web projects), learning design, and exploring new ideas through writing.
